Welcome to LocalCare!

Self-care and treatment are complicated and personal things! Everybody benefits from different techniques, and often our self-care has to be tailored to our locations!

Davis has a lot of wonderful things to offer, but it can be hard to find the things that might be helpful to you in the process of taking care of yourself. With LocalCare (orignally titled Help-Yelp) you can find self-care and treatment solutions and suggestions from around Davis, supplied by locals!

Self-care is personal! Not every suggestion or tip you find here will work for you, and that's okay! Take what you need, and leave the rest!
