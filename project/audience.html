audience research

persona: Jenny

"I want to be able to participate more fully in my life, and I know treatment is an important step towards that goal, but I don't have time with all the work I have to do!"

  • about: Jenny's mental illness adds difficulty to the completion of everyday tasks, but she struggles to find time to treat it or to prioritize her own well-being as a full-time student and on-campus employee. Her academic, professional, and personal responsibilities and activities keep her too busy to seek long-term treatment or practice significant symptom-management.
  • job title: college student, on-campus bookstore employee
  • age: 20
  • gender: female
  • location: suburban, college town
  • goals: manage symptoms of mental illness, find ways to engage more both socially and academically, encorporate self-care techniques into daily routine
  • actions: attempts to seek counselling but struggles to find time, engages in some light mindfulness techniques
